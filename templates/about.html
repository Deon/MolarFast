About MolarFast

MolarFast was created at the University of Waterloo's Software Engineering HackDay on November 21, 2014. MolarFast is designed to give you the Molar Mass of a chemical compound quickly - hence MolarFast. This web-app was developed with AngularJS and Bootstrap for the front-end, with Flask and Python providing the back-end.

About Us
